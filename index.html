Registration for Brainhack Princeton 2020 is now open!

Brainhack Princeton 2020 is an official satellite event of the international Global Brainhack 2020. The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: December 9-11, 2020

Costs: Free for members of the Princeton community

Location: Virtual via Zoom

The theme of this year's hackathon is contributing content to the Princeton Handbook for Reproducible Neuroimaging. However, participants are welcome to work on any project! We are also looking for participants who are willing to lead tutorials (covering any resource you think is useful) during the hackathon.

Please visit the Princeton Brainhack GitHub repository for updated information on the developing hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.

Participants are encouraged to submit project and tutorial ideas here.

Hosted by: Princeton Neuroscience Institute and Department of Psychology
